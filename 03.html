About the internship

Working with WordPress Avada and WooCommerce for modernmediahub.nl involves designing and developing an e-commerce website using the Avada theme and the WooCommerce plugin. The Avada theme is a popular WordPress theme that provides a flexible and customizable design for websites, while the WooCommerce plugin enables the creation of an online store with features such as product pages, shopping carts, and payment processing. My main experiences while building this website were web design and development, as well as I became familiar with WordPress, the Avada theme, and the WooCommerce plugin. In this process I created a custom design for the webshop using the Avada theme, integrating the WooCommerce plugin to enable e-commerce functionality, and configuring the website settings to meet the client’s needs.

Working for modernmediahub.nl involved collaborating with the client (kiteboardschool.nl) to understand their business goals and requirements, and providing ongoing support and maintenance for the website.

Overall, working with WordPress Avada and WooCommerce for modernmediahub.nl involved creating a custom e-commerce website using the Avada theme and the WooCommerce plugin, and providing ongoing support and maintenance to ensure the website meets the client’s (kiteboardschool.nl) needs.
